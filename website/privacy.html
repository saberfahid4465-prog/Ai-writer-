AI Writer — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Writer ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application that generates professional documents using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

AI Writer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers collected
  • No location data accessed
  • No contacts or camera accessed
  • No advertising identifiers used
  • No analytics or tracking services used
  • No cookies used

3. Data That Stays on Your Device

All data created by AI Writer remains exclusively on your device:

  • Generated documents (PDF, Word, PPT, Excel) are saved to your device's local storage
  • App preferences (theme, language) are stored locally via AsyncStorage
  • Token usage counters are stored locally and reset daily
  • File history is stored locally on your device

4. Third-Party API Services

AI Writer communicates with the following external services to provide its functionality:

  • Longcat AI API — Your topic text or uploaded document content is sent to generate, translate, or summarize documents. No personal identifiers are included in these requests.
  • Pexels API — Image keyword searches are sent to find relevant stock photos for your documents. No personal data is included.

All API communications use HTTPS encryption. We do not control the privacy practices of these third-party services.

5. Data Encryption

All data transmitted between the app and external APIs is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing app data from Android Settings → Apps → AI Writer → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)
  • Deleting individual generated files from your device's file manager

7. Children's Privacy

AI Writer is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Permissions

AI Writer requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • INTERNET — Required to communicate with AI and image APIs
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required to check network connectivity

No dangerous permissions (camera, location, contacts, storage) are requested.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
